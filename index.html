About

Born from the tension between authenticity and appearance — UnofficiallyOfficial is for people who choose real over curated.

We make minimalist statement pieces that feel like you. No filters. No permission. Limited production keeps each piece rare and meaningful.

“Real doesn’t need a filter.”

Mission

To build a movement for those tired of performing for others. We empower self-expression through refined, restrained design and short-run drops that reward loyalty — not hype.

  • Small-batch streetwear: hoodies, tees, caps
  • Accessory drops: rings, keychains

Frequently Asked Questions

When is the next drop?

Our next collection launches every March 1. Drops are limited and may not be restocked.

How can I join the waitlist?

Join via the Waitlist page by entering your email. Waitlist members get early access and priority notifications.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes — we ship internationally with tracking on all orders.

Can I return or exchange items?

Returns are accepted within 14 days for unworn items in original condition. Exchanges depend on availability.
